Why is a Thyroid Cancer Prediction System needed?

A Thyroid Cancer Prediction System enables early and accurate detection, improving patient outcomes and reducing diagnostic delays. AI-assisted predictions provide fast, reliable, and explainable support for clinicians.

What is Thyroid Cancer?

Thyroid cancer is a disease in which abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the thyroid gland, a butterfly-shaped gland located in the neck that regulates metabolism and hormone production.
